---
title: One time payments
description: Charge a flat fee for lifetime access or digital downloads
---

Not every product needs a subscription. If you are selling a lifetime software license, a template, or charging a one time setup fee for an AI agent, you use a flat base price.

## Set up a one time price

<Frame>
  <img height="200" src="/images/One-time.png" />
</Frame>

1. Open your draft plan and ensure it is set to Paid
2. Under the billing cycles select One time
3. Enter the total amount you want to charge
4. Click Save

Customers pay this amount exactly once during checkout. If you attach digital downloads or license keys to this plan they gain permanent access to those assets immediately after payment.

## Combine with usage

You can charge a one time setup fee and still bill for ongoing usage. 

Just select One time for the base fee and add your metered features below it. The customer pays the base price upfront to gain access and then pays for their compute or token usage at the end of each billing period.
