We use Stripe to ensure you get paid fast and your tax documents are handled automatically.

## Setup

Before you can receive funds, you need to connect a bank account.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect account">
    Navigate to the **Payouts** group and click the **Set up payout** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete onboarding">
    You will be redirected to a secure Stripe form. Choose your account type (Individual or Company) and enter your banking details.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Dashboard

Once set up, the main Payouts view gives you a real-time snapshot of your cash flow.

<Frame>
  <img height="200" src="/images/payout-dashboard.png" alt="Payout dashboard showing available funds and processing amounts" />
</Frame>

### Key metrics
* **Available to payout**
  The funds that have cleared and are ready to be transferred to your bank.
* **Processing amount**
  Revenue that is currently being processed. You can see the estimated date for when these funds will become available.
* **Billing cycle**
  Shows your current schedule. If funds are available, you can click **Withdraw funds** to initiate a manual transfer immediately.

### Payout history
Below the metrics, you will see a list of every payout we have sent to your bank.
* Check the status of past transfers.
* See the exact date the funds landed.
* View the destination bank account for each transfer.
