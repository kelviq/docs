The **Transactions** tab provides a granular breakdown of every single movement of money. This includes successful payments, refunds, and failures.

## Filtering
You can filter the list to find specific issues.
* **Success:** Completed payments.
* **Refunded:** Money returned to customers.
* **Failed:** Declined cards or failed transfers.

## Transaction details
Click on any transaction row to open the details view. This breaks down the math so you know exactly what you earned from that specific order.

* **Gross total:** The amount the customer paid.
* **Processing fees:** The platform and gateway fees deducted.
* **Net earnings:** The final amount added to your balance.
* **Payment details:** The card or method used.
