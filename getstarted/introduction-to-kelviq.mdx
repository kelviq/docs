##### Kelviq is the complete monetization platform for your SaaS, AI agents, and digital products. It combines a merchant of record, subscription management, and usage-based billing into one system so you can set prices, track variable compute costs, collect payments, and stay compliant with global tax.

---

## Why Kelviq

Monetization is more than just a payment. It's a complex system of pricing models, usage metering, subscription logic, feature access, global tax, and compliance. Building this from scratch takes months of engineering time and ongoing legal overhead.

Kelviq is the complete revenue system that handles all this complexity for you. You can launch any pricing model and sell globally from day one, all while staying compliant.

With Kelviq you can:


- **Get started in minutes** You don’t have to handle dozens of webhooks, build a database layer to track customers, manage subscription states, or hardcode feature access. Kelviq provides a single, unified system.

- **Handle Global Tax & Compliance Automatically** As your Merchant of Record, Kelviq takes on the full liability for global sales tax (VAT, GST, etc.), fraud, and regulatory compliance. You can sell in 100+ countries without ever registering for a foreign tax ID.

- **Run any pricing model** Flat fee, usage, seats, tiers, volume, credits, pay-as-you-go, overage charges, or hybrids. Configure them in minutes.

- **Sell digital products with ease** Securely deliver files, e-books, or software on purchase and automatically generates license keys right after checkout.

- **Meter usage in real time** Track every API request, workflow run, background agent task, or LLM token usage as it happens. Set soft or hard caps, pick reset cycles, send alerts, and handle overages with one call.

- **Control feature access** Define features per plan. Turn them on or off and check access in your app with one SDK call.

- **Localized pricing by country** Offer country-specific prices in 135+ currencies with VPN, proxy, and fraud protection built in. You can also set up promotions for specific countries.

- **Ship pricing changes without touching code** A/B test plans, roll out changes, or update pricing in real time without redeploys.

- **Manage the full customer lifecycle** Handle upgrades, downgrades, migrations, overrides, and grandfathering without writing custom scripts.

## Who kelviq is for

- **AI product companies** — track what your autonomous agents do for each customer and bill based on actual usage. Maintain healthy unit economics as your underlying compute costs scale.

- **Developers** — integrate billing without spending weeks writing complex custom usage code, database sync jobs, or webhook listeners.

- **Product managers** — take ownership of pricing and plans. Run experiments, roll out promotions, and update entitlements directly from the dashboard without waiting for engineering.  

- **Founders** — Launch globally from day one without becoming a tax expert. Kelviq acts as your merchant of record and takes on the full legal liability for VAT, sales tax, and compliance so you can focus on building the product.

- **Digital creators** — Sell your e-books, software, design assets, or courses with zero hassle. Kelviq provides a complete, all-in-one solution that securely delivers your files, generates license keys, and handles all global taxes for you—no code or complex setup required.
