title: "Migrate from Cashfree"
description: "Plan a phased move of your customers and subscriptions from Cashfree to Kelviq"
---

Kelviq supports assisted migrations from Cashfree. We plan the move in phases so you can test the Kelviq setup before moving every active subscription.

## Information to send us

To scope the migration, send Kelviq the current number of:

* Customers
* Active subscriptions
* Products

We use these counts to review the size of the migration and prepare a plan for your account.

## Recommended migration sequence

1. **Review the Cashfree setup.** List the products, prices, billing cycles, active subscriptions, and customer records that need to move.
2. **Create the matching Kelviq catalog.** Set up the products and plans your existing customers will use.
3. **Test in Sandbox mode.** Check checkout, subscription creation, renewals, entitlements, and the Customer Portal.
4. **Move a small group first.** Verify their access, billing dates, and subscription status before continuing.
5. **Move the remaining customers in batches.** Review each batch before starting the next one.
6. **Complete the cutover.** Stop creating new subscriptions in Cashfree after the Kelviq flow has been verified.

<Warning>
  Do not cancel existing Cashfree subscriptions or payment authorizations until Kelviq confirms the cutover steps. Whether saved payment methods or authorizations can be moved depends on your current Cashfree setup. Some customers may need to authorize a payment method again.
</Warning>

## Start the migration

Email [hi@kelviq.com](mailto:hi@kelviq.com) with the three counts above. The Kelviq team will use them to plan the first migration phase with you.
